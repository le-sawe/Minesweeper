Minesweeper Game

How to play ?
  • The mines coordination on the board is random
  • If the clicked square not include a bomb , then you will open the squares until you reach a numbers
  • The number indicate the surrounding bomb number
  • if you are sure that this square include a bomb then you can put a flag by pressing right click {you can't open the square when a there a flag on it}
  • if you suspect the square you can put a question mark on it by pressing right click on the flag square {you can open the square on this situation}
  • if you want to reset the square to normal you have to right click on the quesiton mark square
  • the mine indicator decrease after each flag
  • you can restart the game on the same settings by clicking restart
  • you can change the settings (board width , board height , bomb number) by clicking on Advance settings button
  • you will lose if you open a mine
  • you will win if you discover all the safe square or if you put a flag on every bomb square
Have fun ! :)
